The Journey

From Engineer to Educator to Entrepreneur

Bridging technology and education to empower the next generation of innovators.

Rajath Navas is the visionary Chief Executive Officer of Pace Lab, and a trailblazer in the space of skill development and training. With a BTech and MBA, his journey began with a simple question: Why are so many engineering graduates unprepared for real-world challenges?

That insight sparked a revolution. Under Rajath's leadership, Pace Lab has trained over 30,000 students in just five years — reshaping how India approaches engineering education. From keynote speeches at IITs and ISRO to inspiring 10,000+ students directly, Rajath brings strategy, energy, and empathy to everything he touches.

He's also an active entrepreneur across tech and real estate, and was recognized by Times of India for his innovative startup concepts.
